About

Dr. Yue Ma is currently an Associate Professor at the School of Microelectronics, Nanjing University of Science and Technology. His research group is affiliated with the Key Laboratory of Near-Range Radio Frequency Perception Chips and Microsystems, Ministry of Education, focusing on cutting-edge research in RF perception, array signal processing, covert communication, and electromagnetic control.

Supported by advanced experimental platforms in Jiangbei New District, his group provides systematic research training and engineering practice for graduate students, with access to chip probe testing, EDA design, and RF communication testing facilities.

关于我

现任南京理工大学微电子学院副教授，研究团队隶属于近程射频感知芯片与微系统教育部重点实验室，长期聚焦射频感知、阵列信号处理、隐蔽通信与电磁调控领域研究。

依托江北新区研创园高端实验平台，拥有芯片探针测试、EDA设计、射频通信测试等完备硬件条件，为研究生提供系统化科研训练与工程实践支撑。

团队深度结合国防与民用需求，依托低空智能无人装备协同创新联盟，推动理论研究、芯片设计与工程应用落地结合。
